## 2) 🔧 What you said you'd CHANGE next time (your own regrets)

These are the highest-signal items — straight from your mouth — and they should be **non-negotiable buys for Europe**:

1. **Real cycling shorts / bibs.** You rode the Rockies with **no padded shorts** → "a lot of ass pain," neck pain from 10 h/day on the saddle. *"Definitely I would invest in cycling shorts next time."* (Your `todo.md` already lists **2–3 premium bibs** — Assos/Castelli/Rapha.) ⭐ #1 fix
2. **Compression tights / leg cover.** Only had shorts; got dangerously cold/wet on the Icefields descents. The single thing you most wished you'd packed; your tights are "pretty compressible" so they'd have fit. ⭐
3. **Better / waterproof gloves.** You developed **cycling palsy** (ulnar-nerve numbness in the pinky/outer palm) that lingered **10+ days**; cold and rain aggravated it. ⭐
4. **Fenders/mudguards (or accept a drivetrain service).** No fenders → rain drove grime into the chain/crank; "I need to go for a service for sure." 
5. **Replace the broken saddle bag** with something that won't fail under load.
6. **A working pump** (ditch the leaky Bell).
